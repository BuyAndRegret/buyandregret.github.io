Smite 3.10 Review for consoles -Trgrf

As of this morning the newest update for Smite Patch 3.10 was released at 5am for Playstation 4 and 6am for Xbox One. This update includes the new god Fafnir, a few minor bug fixes and some god changes as well as a pretty huge system added into the game that players have been waiting for. I will be touching on the new system known as Clans it will be short and sweet.

The update has finally brought clans into the game a much wanted and needed feature that many console smite players have been longing for over the past year. The newly integrated clan system brings with it a leveling system specific to the clan itself known as the personal honor system. The personal honor system allows members to accumulate points that go toward the same goal allowing users to unlock exclusive skins. In order to unlock a skin a certain amount of points must be reached within the clans point system but these skins are only available through the clan system. The skins are for select gods in the game and transform them from their normal look to a more sinister and dark one, these skins are known as shadow skins and have special in game effects. With the introduction to clans finally in the game for all console platforms you can expect to see even more players online and ready to grind the night away.

Thanks for reading my short little review for the Smite Console Patch 3.10 and I look forward to all the feedback that is given. Have a wonderful day everyone.
