Xbox Conference news roundup

Another wonderful year at E3 passes us by revealing a huge selection of games and other major news coming from Sony, Microsoft, Bethesda, and Ubisoft. Let's start this all by going through the major news from the Xbox One scene such as games, console upgrades, and controller upgrades. Microsoft started off their E3 conference by announcing the Xbox One S a new console that is 40% smaller than the model everyone has today the release date for the new Xbox One S is sometime in August. Microsoft has given us a teaser for Project Scorpio a new adaptation of the xbox one console which is in development and has a release around Christmas 2017. The new Xbox Play Anywhere service is great if you digitally buy a game on the Xbox One that same game can be played on Windows 10 at no additional cost, cross platform gaming in other words.

The amount of wonderful games that were announced at this year's E3 was more than just a shock to all of us here at BuyandRegret we are super excited to inform you on all the big name titles presented. Gears of War 4 is finally upon us with its extraordinary 8 minute gameplay shown at E3 I can safely say it's one of the top 3 anticipated games coming out, it's release date is on October 11, 2016. After 4 years of waiting Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon Wildlands is said to be released on March 7, 2017. We here at BuyandRegret are extremely excited for Battlefield 1 after seeing the multiplayer gameplay we are looking forward to getting our hands on it, it's said to be release on October 21, 2016. The stealth and cunning of Dishonored is back with Dishonored 2 said to be released on November 11, 2016. For Honor has been on our hit list for quite some time we see it as Chivalry Medieval Warfare on steroids with a new hit target system that brings the game to a whole knew world of possibilities, the game is said to be released by February 14, 2017. The gangsters are back after 6 years of hiding Mafia 3 is almost here said to be released on October 7, 2016. Titanfall 2 if it lives up to its predecessor we could be looking at the future of FPS said to be released sometime in December of this year. We’ve all seen the trailer for Watch Dogs 2 it looks great but the first Watch Dogs gave off the same feeling of how great it was going to be but ended up being a let down so far it looks good hope to see more soon, Watch Dogs 2 will be released on November 15, 2016. Thanks for reading and this has been PsychoticOdious with the news.
